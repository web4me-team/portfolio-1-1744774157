Joe P. Samples

Joe P. Samples is a dedicated researcher with a Ph.D. in Neuroscience and extensive experience in cell and structural biology. He has a strong background in neurobiology, respiratory biology, and cell and molecular biology, with a focus on learning and memory, cell adhesion, and cancer biology. He has held research and teaching positions at prestigious institutions and has been recognized with multiple honors and awards.
